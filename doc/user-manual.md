# User Manual

End-user guide for using VeloxPark parking system.

---

## For Vehicle Owners

### Step 1: Check Vehicle Status

1. **Visit VeloxPark Website**
   - Open your browser
   - Go to VeloxPark URL

2. **Select "I'm a User"**
   - Click "Get Started" on homepage
   - Click "Enter User Portal" button

3. **Enter License Plate**
   - Type your vehicle number (e.g., TS15EL5671)
   - Case doesn't matter (auto-converts to uppercase)
   - Click "Check Status"

### Step 2: View Parking Information

**If Vehicle is Currently Parked:**
- Entry time shown
- Zone location displayed
- Status: "Currently Parked"
- Hourly rate shown

**If Vehicle Has Exited:**
- Entry time and exit time
- Duration (hours & minutes)
- Parking fee calculated
- "Proceed to Payment" button

### Step 3: Make Payment (If Exited)

1. **Click "Proceed to Payment"**
   - Shows amount to pay
   - QR code displayed

2. **Scan QR Code**
   - Open any UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
   - Scan QR code from screen
   - Confirm payment in UPI app

3. **Payment Confirmation**
   - After payment, click "I've Paid"
   - See success message
   - Return home

## Pricing

### Free Parking Period
**First 30 minutes:** FREE ✅

### Paid Parking
**After 30 minutes:** Charged per hour (rounded up)

| Vehicle Type | Rate |
|--------------|------|
| Car | ₹20/hour |
| Bike | ₹10/hour |
| Truck | ₹50/hour |
| EV | ₹20/hour |

**Examples:**
- 25 minutes: ₹0 (free)
- 45 minutes: ₹20 (1 hour)
- 2 hours 15 minutes: ₹60 (3 hours rounded up)

## Frequent Questions

**Q: Can I check status without entering parking?**
A: Yes, enter any license plate to check if it's in the system.

**Q: What if my vehicle doesn't show up?**
A: Double-check the plate number. Contact parking staff if issue persists.

**Q: Can I pay with card/cash?**
A: Currently only UPI payment via QR code. Contact staff for alternatives.

**Q: Is there a parking time limit?**
A: Check with parking management for specific policies.

**Q: What if I lost my parking ticket?**
A: No ticket needed - system is automatic via license plate recognition.

**Q: Can I get a receipt?**
A: UPI payment confirmation serves as receipt. Save in UPI app.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "Vehicle not found"**
- Solution: Check spelling of plate number
- Ensure vehicle is registered in system

**Problem: QR code won't scan**
- Solution: Increase screen brightness
- Try different UPI app
- Ask staff to scan manually

**Problem: Amount seems wrong**
- Solution: Check entry/exit times shown
- Verify parking rate for your vehicle type
- Contact staff if discrepancy
